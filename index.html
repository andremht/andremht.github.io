Highlights

  • As of October 1st, 2015, I became an Assistant Professor at the Faculty of Technology, Policy and Management, Delft University of Technology.

  • Invited speaker at the pre-conference workshop “Smart Cities: Service Models, Vulnerabilities, and Resilience”, held within the 54th IEEE Conference on Decision and Control, Osaka, Japan, Dec. 2015.

  • Co-organizer of the Special session on “Secure and Resilient Industrial Automation and Control Systems” at the 20th IEEE International Conference on Emerging Technologies and Factory Automation, Luxembourg, Sept. 2015.

  • The paper "Distributed Coordination of Household Electricity Consumption" received the Best Student-Paper Award at the IEEE Multi-Conference on Systems and Control 2014.

  • The paper "Attack models and scenarios for networked control systems" (HiCoNS’12) has been listed by ACM as “Notable Computing Books and Articles of 2012”.

  • The paper "On the Optimal Step-size Selection for the Alternating Direction Method of Multipliers" was a Best Student-Paper Award Finalist at NecSys 2012.
