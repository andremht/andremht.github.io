Highlights

  • Our edited book on security and privacy is now available: “Safety, Security and Privacy for Cyber-Physical Systems”, R. M. Ferrari and A. M. H. Teixeira, Eds. Cham: Springer International Publishing, 2021. (link)

  • Anh Tung Nguyen has started his PhD studies at my research group in May 2021. Welcome!
